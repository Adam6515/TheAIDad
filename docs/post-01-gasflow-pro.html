YouTube — Video Description

I Built an AI CRM for Commercial Gas Engineers (Full Story)

In this video I walk through GasFlow Pro — an AI-powered lead management and CRM tool I built specifically for commercial gas engineers.

Coming from a background in gas, electrical and plumbing, I know first-hand how many jobs get lost simply because leads aren't tracked and follow-ups don't happen. This is my attempt to fix that with AI.

What GasFlow Pro does:
- Scrapes Google Maps to find commercial gas business leads automatically
- Pipeline cards to manage and track every deal
- Activities page to log every interaction
- Certificate management with expiry due dates
- AI enrichment using Claude Sonnet 4.6 to find contact details and business background

How I built it:
- Base code built in Emergent AI
- Exported to Claude Code for deeper functionality and bug fixes
- Google Auth and API integration
- Frontend and backend hosted on Vercel
- Database and AI enrichment layer added after initial build
